## Design Tips for High-Ticket Conversion

âœ… **DO:**
- Use professional fonts (Inter, Poppins, Montserrat)
- Keep it clean and minimal (not cluttered)
- Use high contrast (dark bg + white text or vice versa)
- Include the price "â‚¬2,000" (anchors expectations)
- Show time savings visually ("3 seconds" vs "2 minutes")

âŒ **DON'T:**
- Use comic sans or unprofessional fonts
- Cram too much text (mobile users can't read small text)
- Use stock photos of fake "tech" (circuits, matrix code, etc.)
- Hide the price (high-ticket buyers want to know upfront)
- Use clickbait (damages trust)

## Testing Your Creatives

Before launching ads, test:

1. **Mobile preview** â€” 70% of Meta traffic is mobile
   - Text readable at thumbnail size?
   - CTA button obvious?
   
2. **3-second test** â€” Show to a friend for 3 seconds
   - Can they explain what it's selling?
   - If not, simplify

3. **Thumbnail test** â€” View at 200x200px
   - Still compelling when small?
   - Text still readable?
